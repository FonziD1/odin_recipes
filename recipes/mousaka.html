Recipe Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Slice the eggplant and sprinkle with salt. Let it sit for 30 minutes to draw out moisture.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef or lamb with chopped onion and minced garlic until browned.
  4. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a baking dish, layer eggplant slices, meat sauce, and béchamel sauce. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with remaining béchamel sauce and bake for 45 minutes.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
